## How to Use This Library

1. **Filter** by your stage and budget first
2. **Pick 2-3** tactics maximum to start (focus beats breadth)
3. **Give each 90 days** before judging (marketing compounds)
4. **Document** what works and double down
5. **Rotate** low-performers out after fair test, add new experiments
